The body mass index (BMI) is a measure that uses your height and weight to work out if your weight is healthy.
For most adults (20 years old and older), an ideal BMI is in the 18.5 to 24.9 range.

If the result of the body mass index determines:
Below 18.5. You are in the category: Underweight.
Between 18.5 – 24.9. You are in the category: Healthy Weight
Between 25.0 – 29.9. You are in the category: Overweight
Between 30.0 and Above. You are in the category: Obesity

WARNING!

This is not a medical advice. Remember, each body type is different.
If you have doubts according to your BMI, or you want to improve your health,
PLEASE visit your doctor for a complete exam and precise information.
